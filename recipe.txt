Seven Onion BBQ Meatloaf Recipe

INGREDIENTS:

1 package Seven Onion BBQ Meatloaf Mix
2 lbs. ground beef
2/3 cup bread crumbs*
2/3 cup water
2 eggs
1 tsp. black pepper
2.5 fl. oz. Midwestern Sweet Barbecue Sauce

*For a gluten-free meatloaf, use gluten-free bread crumbs.

DIRECTIONS:

1 - Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2 - Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with nonstick cooking spray
3 - Add Seven Onion BBQ Meatloaf Mix, ground beef, bread crumbs, water, eggs, salt, and pepper in a large bowl and mix gently, just until combined.
5 - Scoop mixture onto baking sheet and form an 8x4-inch loaf. 
6 - Bake uncovered for 50 min. 
7 - Brush liberally with Midwestern Sweet Barbecue Sauce and bake for an additional 10 min. 
8 - Internal temperature of meat should be at least 160 degrees. 
9 - Rest meatloaf for 10 min. before slicing and serving.

NOTES:

Serve with: Mashed potatoes, green beans, or your favorite side dish.

YIELDS:

Serves 4
